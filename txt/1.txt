This APP supports account cancellation via mobile phone number. The specific operation process is as follows:
Open the APP, click the "My" option in the lower right corner of the interface to enter the personal center page. On the personal center page, find and click the "Settings" button. After entering the settings page, click the "Account and Security" option.  On the Account and Security page, click the "Cancel Account" option. An identity verification interface will pop up. Enter the mobile phone number used to register the account and click "Get Verification Code." Accurately fill in the received verification code in the corresponding field to complete the identity verification. After successful verification, the page will display relevant prompts and precautions for account cancellation. Please read them carefully. After confirming everything is correct, click the "Confirm Cancellation" button to submit the account cancellation application. II. Account Cancellation Conditions The account is in a secure state, with no recent logins from other locations or abnormal operations posing security risks. If the system detects security risks, it will prompt you to resolve the relevant issues first. Cancellation can only be performed after the account's security status has returned to normal. There are no outstanding orders, transactions, or other business transactions in the account. If there are outstanding orders, you need to complete them first (such as canceling orders, completing payments, confirming receipt, etc.), or contact customer service for assistance. Only after all business transactions in the account are completed can you submit a cancellation application. The account's binding relationship with third-party platforms has been completely severed.  If your APP account is linked to third-party platforms such as WeChat, QQ, or Alipay, you must unlink the account from this APP in the settings of the respective third-party platform before canceling your APP account to avoid information residue or subsequent problems caused by the linking. III. Account Cancellation Result Feedback After submitting your account cancellation application, we will complete the review and processing within 15 business days and send the cancellation result to the mobile phone number you provided during registration via SMS. You can also check the cancellation progress and result on the "Account and Security" page within the APP. If your account cancellation application is approved, your account will be officially cancelled, and you will no longer be able to log in to this APP with that account. Personal information associated with that account (such as account information, history, favorites, etc.) will be deleted or anonymized in accordance with relevant laws and regulations and this APP's privacy policy to protect your privacy to the greatest extent possible. If your account cancellation application is not approved, we will inform you of the reason for the failure via SMS and in-app messages. You can make corresponding adjustments according to the prompts or contact customer service for solutions.  IV. Impact of Account Cancellation Once your account is successfully cancelled, you will not be able to recover it, log in to this app again, or retrieve any historical data or information within the account. Therefore, please ensure you have backed up important data, such as chat logs, personal content, and important files, before cancelling your account. After account cancellation, any user agreements or authorization agreements you have signed with this app will automatically terminate, and you will no longer enjoy any services and rights provided by this app. If you have previously used this app account to log in to other related applications or services, account cancellation may affect your use of those applications or services. Please refer to the relevant regulations of the corresponding application or service for specific impacts. If you encounter any problems or need assistance during the account cancellation process, please contact our customer service team at any time.
